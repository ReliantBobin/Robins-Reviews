Borderlands 4

12,063 negative reviews on Steam since it’s release on the 12th of September, so much for a AAA game Gearbox? Honestly though it is a pretty fun game to play and have some down time. One of my favourite features added has to be the gliding, gliding has given a whole new movement feature allowing you to fly across massive gaps and exploring different places you might not have thought to look in previous games, another movement feature added is dodging which is pretty self-explanatory but allows the player to move in quick but short movements to dodge attacks that might’ve been your last hit before “Fight for your life”.

Let’s just hope Gearbox manage to fix the optimisation issue soon though, I could do with some more frames ☹.
